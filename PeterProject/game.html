Entertainment Items

Aquatic Sports

People can find many water sports which they like. No worried if you have no experience about it, we have the best coach here and the teaching is for free. Water scooter, water ski, scuba diving, surfing is popular for young people, and there is a large water park for children. The only thing you need to do this to buy an “Aquatic” ticket. The validity of the ticket is 24 hours.

Land Sports

People can find many land sports and activities which they like. Such as horse riding，bungee jumping, sand skiing, hiking and camping. We will provide the short training for each items and visitor do not need to pay extra fees. The only thing you need to do this to buy an “Land” ticket. The validity of the ticket is 24 hours.

Air Sports

People can find many air sports which they like. Such as skydiving, hot-air balloon, air-racing, gilding, wingsuit flying. No worried if you have no experience about it, we have the best coach here and the teaching is for free. The only thing you need to do this to buy an “Air” ticket. The validity of the ticket is 24 hours.

Leisure

The main part of leisure is SPA project. We have different kind of treatments. Aromatherapy, bathing (artificial waterfall, feet bath, hot spring, hot tub, mud bath, sauna, stream bath, Vichy shower), body wraps, massage, hair, nail care and waxing. People just need to buy a “Leisure” ticket and the validity of the ticket is 48 hours. !

Ball Games

People can find many ball games which they like. Such as golf, table tennis, billiards, bowling and beach volleyball. Everything there is totally free.
